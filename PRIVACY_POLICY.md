# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZyroSafe collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the application, dashboard, or related services.

## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following categories of information:

### Account Information
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password or authentication tokens managed by the identity provider
- Profile information you choose to provide

### Usage and Operational Information
- Login and session activity
- Page and feature usage
- Claim trigger actions
- Transaction status and timestamps
- Fraud risk and approval status

### Location Information
- Live GPS coordinates
- Approximate area, locality, city, and state
- Accuracy data from the device or browser
- IP-based approximate location

### Device and Technical Information
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- IP address
- Session identifiers
- Error logs and diagnostic information

### Financial and Eligibility Information
- Weekly income estimate
- Working hours
- Selected plan
- Trigger-related payout calculations

## 2. How We Use Information
We may use the information collected to:
- Provide the service
- Verify identity and session state
- Calculate payout eligibility
- Detect fraud and abuse
- Show live weather and location context
- Maintain transaction history and audit records
- Improve system reliability and support
- Comply with legal or security obligations

## 3. Location Data Use
Location data is used to:
- Display your current area or locality
- Compare GPS data with IP-based data for fraud detection
- Validate whether a disruption applies to your operational region
- Support claim evaluation and audit trails

You may need to grant location permission for the service to work as intended.

## 4. Weather and External Data
The application may request weather, geocoding, and related contextual data from external providers. These services may receive your approximate coordinates or related query data depending on the request.

## 5. How We Share Information
We may share information with:
- Authentication providers
- Database and hosting providers
- Weather and geocoding providers
- Analytics or monitoring tools
- Support or fraud review personnel when needed

We do not sell your personal information in this draft unless you choose to add such a disclosure later.

## 6. Data Storage
Information may be stored in one or more systems, including:
- Primary database storage
- Fallback storage when the primary system is unavailable
- Session storage for active use
- Audit or transaction logs

Storage duration should be defined in your retention policy.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures to protect information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Retention
We retain information only as long as needed for service delivery, legal obligations, dispute handling, fraud prevention, and audit purposes, subject to the separate retention policy.

## 9. Your Choices
Depending on how the service is configured, you may be able to:
- Update your profile
- Review transaction history
- Sign out of your account
- Disable location permissions in your browser or device

Disabling certain permissions may limit functionality.

## 10. Children
This service is intended for adults or authorized users only. It is not designed for children under the age required by applicable law.

## 11. International Use
If the service is used across regions, information may be processed in other countries where infrastructure or providers are located.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. The effective date should be updated when changes are published.

## 13. Contact
For privacy questions, contact the project owner or the official support channel for the application.

This document is a working draft intended for review and editing. It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before publication.
